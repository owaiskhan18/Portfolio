My Journey

I began my journey as a web developer back in 2017 after completing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. Since then, I've worked with various startups and established companies, helping them create stunning digital experiences for their users.

My approach to development is user-centric - I believe that technology should serve people, not the other way around. This philosophy drives me to create interfaces that are not only visually appealing but also intuitive and accessible to everyone.
